Our Journey

2020

ImaniCredit was founded with a mission to revolutionize lending in Kenya and make financial services accessible to all.

2021

Launched mobile app and expanded to serve over 10,000 customers across Kenya with KES 100M+ in loans disbursed.

2022

Received central bank approval and expanded lending products. Reached 25,000+ active customers and KES 500M+ in total disbursements.

2023

Introduced AI-powered credit scoring and expanded partnership network. Serving 40,000+ customers across 15 counties.

2024

Achieved KES 2B+ total disbursements, expanded to business loans, and launched our latest digital platform for seamless lending.
